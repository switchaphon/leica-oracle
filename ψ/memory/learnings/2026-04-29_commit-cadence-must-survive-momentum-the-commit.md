# Commit cadence must survive momentum. The "commit early" lesson was written at 1

Commit cadence must survive momentum. The "commit early" lesson was written at 12:15 and violated by 13:00 the same day — 5 hours, 4 sessions, 1,800 lines, zero commits. Momentum makes stopping feel like friction, but that's exactly when commits matter most. Fix: make commits mechanical (after every browser verify → commit), not discretionary.
