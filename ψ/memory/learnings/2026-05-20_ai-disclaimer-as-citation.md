# AI disclaimer belongs at the bottom as citation, not at the top as header

**Date**: 2026-05-20
**Context**: AI Health Snapshot in OPD sidebar

## Pattern

When showing AI-generated summaries in medical/clinical UI, the attribution ("AI Health Snapshot", "Generated by AI") should be positioned as a footnote/citation at the bottom — small, gray, right-aligned. Never as a header or prominent banner at the top.

## Why

In clinical context, the vet needs to scan the medical data first. An AI attribution header competes for attention with the actual content. Moving it to a footer citation preserves the information hierarchy: data > source.
