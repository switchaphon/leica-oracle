# PM Oracle owns execution, not just coordination. Spawn team-agents within your s

PM Oracle owns execution, not just coordination. Spawn team-agents within your session to do the work — they inherit project context. Specialist oracles (Chrome, Neon, Pixel, etc.) are consultants only — consult them via maw hey when stuck on domain expertise, but never send them implementation tasks. They lack project context and would have to re-learn the codebase. spawn > delegate, always.
